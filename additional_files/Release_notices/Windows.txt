Installation:
 
 This folder includes two files KandVST.dll and KandVST.vst3 and you can choose to use either one of them.

 KandVST.dll is a vst2 plugin and should be moved to your DAW vst2 folder.
 
 KandVST.vst3 is a vst3 plugin and  should be installed under the folder C:\Program Files\Common Files\VST3\
